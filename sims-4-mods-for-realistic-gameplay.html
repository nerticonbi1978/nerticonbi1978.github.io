  • Trampoline- Your sims get to play on a trampoline!

  • First Love - Children who have a very high friendship have the option to become First Loves, and declare their crushes to each other.
  • Kids Can Walk Dogs- Self explanatory lol.
  • Gift Giving- You can give other Sims gifts, or host your own gift-giving party.
  • Miscarriage/Abortion - Gives you the ability to have miscarriages, and also gives the possibility of abortions.
  • Movie Event- Allows you to host a movie event where your sims can watch a film together and complete movie-related tasks.
  • Quiet When Watching Movie- Your sims will be less inclined to talk/change seats when watching a movie.
